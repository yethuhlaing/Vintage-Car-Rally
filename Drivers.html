Scott Ronald Dixon CNZM is a talented New Zealand racer. Since joining IndyCar, Dixon has won the championship in 2003, 2008, 2013, 2015, 2018, and 2020. Scott Dixon may not be the most popular driver in the IndyCar industry, but he’s probably one of IndyCar’s best. Prost

Alain Prost is France’s most famous race car driver. Form four-time formula One Champion, Prost has the second most F1 titles of all time. Also, from 1987 to 2001, he held the record for most Grand Prix victories in history, which is impressive enough.

Nikolaus

Andreas Nikolaus Lauda was an Austrian F1 racer as well as an entrepreneur. He was the F1 World Drivers’ Champion three times, in 1975, 1977, and 1984. Also, he was the only driver in F1 history ever to win both Ferrari and McLaren, the most famous producers in the sport.

Johnson

As a current race car driver, some people might also feel premature to include Jimmie Johnson on the list. However, Johnson has achieved a lot in his career so far. The American race car driver has already won the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series six times.

Fisichella

Giancarlo Fisichella is a well-known driver in Formula One of Minardi, Jordan, Benetton, Sauber, Renault, India Force, and Ferrari. He is also known as Fisico, Giano, or Fisi, is a talented Italian race car driver. He could boast of the dramatic speed change in his day. But the problem with ‘Fisi’ is that he has never been reliable enough like Alonso or Hamilton to start a good season together.

Vettel

Sebastian Vettel is a German racing car driver to competes in Formula One. In addition, he is a four-time world champion of Formula One. He has won consecutive titles with Red Bull Racing from 2010 to 2013. And he is considered one of the best drivers in the history of the sport.
